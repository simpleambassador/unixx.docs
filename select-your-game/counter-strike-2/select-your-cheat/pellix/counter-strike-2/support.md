## Common Issues

1. Loader crashes with no error
   * Sync your system time (use time.windows.com, time.cloudflare.com, or time.nist.gov)
   * Install [vc\_redist](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=48145) and restart your PC
2. Loader stuck on loading
   * Try using a VPN or disabling antivirus (ESET confirmed to cause issues)
   * Restart your PC
3.  Menu clicks not working

    * Check for Interception driver in the cheat console
    * Remove it using the Interception.zip tool and restart your PC
