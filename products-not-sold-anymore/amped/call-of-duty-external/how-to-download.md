1. Disable Virtualization Based Security, Kernel DMA Security, and TPM in your BIOS.
2. Disable Windows Defender completely using Defender Control: [Download link](https://www.sordum.org/9480/defender-control-v2-1/).
3. Disable any external antivirus software to avoid conflicts with driver loading or injection.
4. Install C++ Redistributables and DirectX Runtimes.
5. Download Riva Tuner for overlay functionality: [Download Here](https://www.guru3d.com/download/rtss-rivatuner-statistics-server-download/).

7. Open Riva Tuner as Administrator.
8. Run loader.exe as Administrator, then log in with your account. If you're new, register and enter your license key.
9. Wait for the injection process to complete, following any prompts provided by the loader.
10. After successful injection, open your selected game and enjoy!
